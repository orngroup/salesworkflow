Phase 1 Enquiry & first contact
Sales
Sales Expert · Senior Sales · Branch Manager
01
Group enquiry received
WhatsApp · call · walk-in · referral
Sales Expert
›
Enquiry ≠ booking. No system entry, no inventory block. Sales Expert logs interest only. Groups over 15 pax reviewed by Senior Sales. Min group size applies — smaller enquiries redirect to FIT track.

Sales Expert duties at enquiry: Identify departure date interest · note approx pax count · check departure availability · send product brochure · log in CRM within 24hrs.
02
Ground reality disclosure — 14 items
Hotels · rooms · meals · group timings — all disclosed before payment
Sales ExpertBooking Owner
›
14 mandatory group disclosures:
  • Hotels may be outside city centre / highway zone
  • Triple rooms may be double + sofa/extra bed
  • Some meals may be packed (early starts, long drives)
  • Group timings are fixed — individual flexibility limited
  • Walking required at many European sites
  • Transfers are common group — private is extra
  • Early check-in / late checkout not guaranteed
  • Flights may have layovers, airline may change
  • Optionals cost extra unless written in package
  • Room upgrades / views not guaranteed
  • Jain / special dietary must be pre-requested
  • Coach access restricted at some attractions
  • Tour Manager runs the group — no private customisation
  • Special requests need written approval — not auto-guaranteed
Customer signs acknowledgement. Missing = Sales-side failure.

01
Individual enquiry received
Solo · couple · family · custom itinerary request
FIT Sales Expert
FIT — individual pax, no group departure
›
FIT bookings are individual or small-group (1–6 pax typically) with custom or semi-custom itineraries. No fixed group departure. Sales Expert is Product Consultant — builds the itinerary around the customer's dates and preferences.

FIT Sales Expert duties at enquiry: Understand travel dates · pax profile · budget bracket · customisation requirements · flight preferences · visa situation · log in CRM as FIT enquiry (separate from group pipeline).
02
Ground reality disclosure — adapted for FIT
Hotels · transfers · meals · flexibility limits
FIT Sales Expert
FIT — more flexibility but stricter supplier commitment rules
›
FIT disclosures overlap with group but key differences:
  • More flexibility on dates, hotels, room types — but all flexibility must be costed and confirmed in writing
  • Supplier availability is not guaranteed until booking deposit is received and supplier is blocked
  • Hotel check-in/out times: FIT may have better options but still not guaranteed without a paid supplement
  • Private transfers standard for FIT — but route, timing, vehicle type must be specified in writing
  • "Customisation" means what is written in the final itinerary. No verbal add-ons.
Same signature requirement as group — acknowledgement on file before payment.

Driver / Supplier
Driver · Supplier Representative · Finance Executive · Ops Controller
07a
Driver / supplier float issued
Pre-tour float advanced (e.g. €500) · recorded in LERMS · driver signs receipt
DriverFinance ExecutiveOps Controller
›
Before departure, Ops Controller approves a pre-tour float to the driver / supplier (typically €300–€500 or as agreed per tour type). Finance Executive records the float in LERMS against the specific tour code and departure date.

Float issue process:
  • Ops Controller submits float request with tour code, driver name, amount, justification
  • Finance Manager approves amounts above standard float threshold
  • Driver signs float receipt — amount, date, tour code, return-by date
  • Float recorded in LERMS as "Advanced to Supplier — [Tour Code] — [Driver Name]"
  • Zero float advances without Ops Controller authorisation
07b
Driver submits expense invoice post-tour
Receipts + invoice submitted within 48 hrs of tour end · verified against float
DriverOps Controller (verifies)Finance Executive (settles)
›
Driver expense submission rules:
  • All receipts must be original (or clear photos) — no receipt = expense not approved
  • Expenses must be submitted within 48 hours of tour end
  • Expense categories: parking · road tolls (above agreed daily cap) · fuel top-up (if applicable) · emergency repairs (pre-approved by Ops) · ferry/tunnel (if not in contract)
  • Personal expenses (meals, accommodation where included) are not reimbursable via the float
Invoice settlement flow:
  • Driver submits: expense invoice + receipts + signed LERMS return form
  • Ops Controller verifies: expenses against approved categories · receipts match amounts · tour dates correct
  • Finance Executive reconciles: actual expenses vs float advanced
  • If actual < float: Driver returns balance. Ops Controller records surplus return in LERMS.
  • If actual > float: Ops Controller approves overage (within 10% buffer) → Finance pays difference. Over 10% requires Finance Manager sign-off.
  • Missing receipts: Expense rejected for that line. Driver cannot claim without receipt.
LERMS close-out: Finance Executive marks float as "Settled" with date and actual vs advanced summary. Any disputed items escalated to Ops Manager → Finance Manager within 5 working days.
⊘
Gate E — Final payment gate · zero dues · all booking types
HARD STOP ›
Owner: Finance Manager + Booking Owner / Consultant / Account Manager
Condition: 100% package + TCS + supplements + add-ons received. Dues = zero.
Exception: Finance Manager + Business Head written approval only.
Consequence: Vouchers released with outstanding dues = non-negotiable violation (§26). Scorecard entry.

B2B exception: Agent credit terms may allow net settlement post-tour — but agent must be within credit limit and terms must be in the signed agreement. Finance Manager must confirm before file proceeds.

Non-negotiable violations — §26 · zero tolerance
ZERO TOLERANCE ›
  • Unrecorded discount or special price given to customer
  • Verbal inclusion commitment not in writing
  • Booking marked confirmed without payment received
  • Dirty or incomplete file pushed to Operations
  • Booking Owner responsibility transferred without documented hand-off note
  • Agent booking confirmed without signed written terms
  • No visa feasibility confirmed for late Europe booking
  • Vouchers released with outstanding dues
  • Special request guaranteed to customer without written approval on file
  • No ground reality disclosure before deposit collected
  • Driver float issued without LERMS entry and signed receipt
  • Driver expense reimbursed without original receipt
